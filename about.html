Company Overview

A registered LLP engaged in building construction

Uprising Infra LLP was established on 19th June 2020 and is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra. The firm operates as a Limited Liability Partnership engaged in building construction within the broader construction sector, registered under ROC Mumbai I with LLPIN AAS-6610.

Our work spans residential and commercial building construction together with infrastructure and civil works — giving clients a single, accountable partner for projects that require both structural and site-level execution.
